# AI Daddy Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 13, 2026

## Introduction

AI Daddy ("we," "our," or "the app") is a supportive AI companion application designed to provide caring reminders, emotional support, and conversational assistance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Chat Messages**: Your conversations with AI Daddy are stored locally on your device
- **User Profile**: Nickname, preferences, and settings
- **Emotional Data**: Mood indicators, stress levels, and emotional patterns derived from your interactions
- **Care Threads**: Health status, travel plans, study schedules, and life events you share

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App interaction patterns, feature usage, and reminder response rates
- **Device Information**: Android version, device model, app version
- **Notification Data**: Reminder scheduling and delivery status

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
- **All personal data is stored locally on your device**
- Chat history, emotional profiles, and care threads remain on your device
- No personal conversations are transmitted to external servers except for AI processing

### AI Processing
- Chat messages are sent to **LongCat AI API** (https://api.longcat.chat) for generating responses
- Messages are processed in real-time and not permanently stored by the AI service
- The AI service uses HTTPS encryption for secure transmission

### App Functionality
- **Emotional Modeling**: Analyze conversation patterns to provide appropriate support
- **Reminder Generation**: Create personalized, contextual reminders based on your needs
- **Care Thread Management**: Track ongoing life situations to provide continued support

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All data is stored in SQLite database on your device
- Data persists until you uninstall the app or manually delete it
- No cloud backup or synchronization

### Security Measures
- HTTPS encryption for all network communications
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- No advertising networks
- No user accounts or authentication required

## Data Sharing

### Third-Party Services
- **LongCat AI**: Chat messages are sent to LongCat AI for generating conversational responses
  - Privacy Policy: https://longcat.chat/privacy
  - Data is processed but not stored permanently
  - No personal identifying information is shared

### No Other Sharing
- We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information
- We do NOT share data with advertisers or marketing companies
- We do NOT use analytics services that track user behavior

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access
- All your data is accessible within the app
- View chat history, emotional state, and care threads in the app interface

### Data Deletion
- **Clear Chat History**: Delete individual or all conversations in Settings
- **Reset App Data**: Clear all data including emotional profiles and care threads
- **Uninstall**: Removes all app data from your device permanently

### Notification Control
- Enable/disable reminders in Settings
- Customize reminder frequency
- Control notification permissions in device settings

## Children's Privacy

AI Daddy is intended for users aged **13 and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## Not a Medical Service

**IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:**

AI Daddy is a supportive companion app and is **NOT**:
- A substitute for professional mental health care
- A medical device or diagnostic tool
- A crisis intervention service
- Qualified to provide medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice

**If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact:**
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988 (US)
- Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
- International: https://findahelpline.com/
- Or call emergency services (911 in US)

## Data Retention

- **Chat messages**: Retained until you delete them or uninstall the app
- **Emotional profiles**: Updated continuously, reset when you clear app data
- **Care threads**: Automatically expire after resolution or 30 days of inactivity
- **Reminders**: Deleted after delivery or when you cancel them
